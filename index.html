Project: QuickDraw

Java/AWS/DynamoDB/JSoup : individual

QuickDraw makes phlebotomy quicker and less error prone by visually mapping test orders to specimen containers. This is achieved from the automated extraction of information from an online medical test directory and storing it in Amazon Web Service's DynamoDB. Querying on test orders delivers the information for a successful blood draw.

Project: NoSQL Comparison

MongoDB/Neo4j : group

My group used ~10^6 crime records from the City of Chicago to build single node instances of the NoSQL databases MongoDB and Neo4j. We designed schema, tested query execution times, optimized performance and documented the qualitative experience. The two databases performed similarly provided they were indexed.

Project: Gnuplot Automation

Gnuplot/Java : individual

While taking a software engineering course focused on the model, simulate, visualize, and analyze paradigm, I realized visualizing dozens of graphs in Excel took too long. Using gnuplot and leveraging OOP principles allowed me to create a flexible, automated process for creating graphs.
